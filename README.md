Unfortunately, that NAS felt Thor's rath during a rather intense storm and short circuted. I then began storing my notes in the Kali VMs I worked off of, primarily the VM on my laptop. Then, while doing some physical tinkering with my laptop, I inadvertently electrocuted the SSD and lost my entire OS, including the VM were I stored all my notes. So, having been forced to build my notes from the ground up for a third time, I have chosen to host everything on GitHub. I hope this will be a safer and more stable option.
